FAQ

Common questions

What is n8n? +
n8n is a powerful open-source workflow automation tool — similar to Zapier or Make, but far more flexible and capable. It allows complex multi-step logic, custom code, and deep integrations.
Can it connect to my existing tools? +
Yes. n8n connects to hundreds of services including Google Workspace, Slack, Notion, Airtable, Salesforce, HubSpot, email providers, databases, and any tool with an API or webhook.
Will the automation run on its own? +
Yes — once deployed, your workflow runs automatically based on the triggers you define. No manual intervention needed.
What if something breaks? +
I build in error handling and notifications so you'll know immediately if something goes wrong. I also offer a free bug-fix period after delivery.
Can you build AI-powered workflows? +
Absolutely. I can integrate AI language models (Claude, GPT) into your workflows to add intelligent decision-making, text processing, summarization, and more.
